## 📖 Overview

The **Prism Insurance Pvt. Ltd. Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution designed to provide a comprehensive view of insurance operations, including policy performance, premium collections, claim activities, and customer demographics. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Premium Amount | 5.98M |
| Coverage Amount | 600.55M |
| Claim Amount | 16.91M |

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of the overall insurance portfolio.
